Karnataka - One State Many Worlds

Karnataka, situated in the southwestern part of India, is a land of striking diversity in both its natural landscapes and its cultural heritage. Stretching from the lush, rain-fed slopes of the Western Ghats to the dry, expansive plains of the Deccan Plateau, and further to the serene, sandy shores along the Arabian Sea, the state offers an impressive range of terrains and climates. It shares borders with Maharashtra and Goa to the north and northwest, Kerala to the southwest, Tamil Nadu to the southeast, and Andhra Pradesh and Telangana to the east. This central positioning has allowed Karnataka to absorb and blend influences from different parts of India, creating a unique cultural mosaic.
