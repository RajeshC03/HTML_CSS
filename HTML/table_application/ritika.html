Ritika Sajdeh

Ritika Sajdeh is a sports manager and the wife of Indian cricketer Rohit Sharma. She has been instrumental in managing Rohit's career and is known for her support and dedication.

Biography

Ritika Sajdeh was born on December 21, 1987, in Mumbai, India. She completed her education in commerce and later pursued a career in sports management. Ritika and Rohit Sharma got married on December 13, 2015, in a grand ceremony attended by many celebrities.

Career

Ritika has worked with several sports management companies and has been involved in organizing various sports events. Her expertise in managing athletes has made her a respected figure in the sports community.

Personal Life

Ritika and Rohit Sharma welcomed their first child, a daughter named Samaira Sharma, on December 30, 2018. Ritika is known for her philanthropic work and often participates in charitable events, supporting various causes.

Fun Fact

Ritika is often seen cheering for Rohit during matches and is a constant source of motivation for him. She has a keen interest in fitness and often shares workout routines on social media.
