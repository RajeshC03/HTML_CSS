Anushka Sharma

Anushka Sharma is a renowned Indian actress and film producer known for her work in Hindi cinema. She has received several awards, including Filmfare Awards, and is one of the highest-paid actresses in India.

Biography

Anushka Sharma was born on May 1, 1988, in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India. She made her acting debut in 2008 with the film "Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi" opposite Shah Rukh Khan.
